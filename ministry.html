Our Management

EMİN ELDAR OĞLU ƏMRULLAYEV

Minister
FULL BIOGRAPHY

Jeyhun Bayramov was born in Baku in 1973. He received his degree in economics from Azerbaijan State University of Economics and degree in law from Azerbaijan University.

He served as the Chief of Staff of the Ministry of Education from 30 May to 12 August 2013. He was appointed as the Deputy of the Minister of Education by the Decree of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan of 12.08.2013.

He was appointed as the Minister of Education by the Decree of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan of 23.04.2018.
